## Pheonix
Nagaji, ♀, 25, Neutral

HP: 44 \
AC: 15 (Breastplate) \
Speed: 30ft \
Prof: +2 \
STR: 16 (+3) \
DEX: 13 (+1) \
CON: 14 (+2) \
INT: 11 (+0) \
WIS: 11 (+1) \
CHA: 14 (+2)

#### Proficiencies
Skills: Athletics, Perception, Intimidation, Stealth \
Resistances: Fire \
Immunities: Poison, Poisoned \
Saves: Strength, Constitution \
Languages: Common, Celestial, Draconic

#### Attacks
Spiked Chain: +6 to hit (Reach), 1d10 + 3 piercing damage (Push or Topple) \
Dagger (2): +6 to hit (20/60), 1d4 + 3 piercing damage (Nick) \
Constrict: +6 to hit, 1d6 + 3 bludgeoning damage + Grapples + Restrains Target \
Bite: +6 to hit, 1d4 + 3 piercing + 1d4 poison damage \
Psionic Strike: Spend 1 Psi Die to increase damage by 1d8 + 2

#### Bonus Actions
Second Wind (4/LR + 1/SR): Heal 1d10 + 5 HP & move 15ft without provoking Attacks of Opportunity \
Action Surge (1/SR): Take an additional Action this turn \
Dragon’s Terror: DC 13 Wisdom Save vs Frightened

#### Reactions
Interception: Within 10ft, reduce ally's damage by 1d10 + 3 \
Protective Field: Spend 1 Psi Die to reduce damage to yourself or ally by 1d8 + 2

#### Psi Warrior 3
Psionic Energy Dice: 6d8 / LR + 1d8 / SR \
Telekinetic Movement (1/SR + 1/PD). You can move an object or a willing creature within 30ft with your chain. You transport the target up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. If the target is Tiny, you can transport it to or from your hand. \
Tactical Mind: You can use Second Wind to increase an ability check. On a failure, the use is not expended. 

### Attraction
Starting: 21 \
Turn Ons: 
- Someone Mysterious: +1d8 Attration on successful Stealth & Sleight of Hand checks
- Complements: +1d8 Attraction when complemented
- Someone Competent: +1d4 Attraction on any successful Skill Checks

Pet Peeves:
- Procrastinators: -1d6 Attraction on lowest initiative
- Making Matters Worse: -1d6 Attraction on failed Insight or Persuasion by 5 or more.
- No Will Power: -1d6 Attraction on failed Wisdom Save
