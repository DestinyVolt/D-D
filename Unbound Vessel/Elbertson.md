## Elbertson
Giant Rat \
Size: Large (for a rodent)

HP: 32 \
AC: 12 (Unarmored Defense) \
Prof: +2 \
Speed: 30rft \
STR: 16 (+3) \
DEX: 10 (+0) \
CON: 14 (+2) \
INT: 4 (-3) \
WIS: 10 (+0) \
CHA: 4 (-3)

***Skills***: Athletics, Intimidation \
***Tools***: Chef's Tools \
***Languages***: Rodent

### Actions: 
- Bite: +5 to hit, 1d4 + 3 piercing damage
- Grapple: +5 to grapple
- Improvised Weapon (20rft / 60rft): +3 to hit, 1d4 + 3 damage

### Bonus Actions:
Frenzy (1 minute, 3/LR): \
\- Resist Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage \
\- +2 Damage to all attacks \
\- Can make an additional attack as a Bonus Action \
\- Immunity to the Frightened Condition \
\- Gain 1 level of exhaustion when your Frenzy ends

### Racial Traits
**Danger Sense.** You have advantage on Dexterity saving throws against effects that you can see. \
**Pack Tactics.** You count as one size larger when determining your carrying capacity and the weight you can push, drag, or lift.
